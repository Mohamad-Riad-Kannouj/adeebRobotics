About the Founder

Omar Abdul-hadi

Omar Abdul-hadi received his PhD in robotics from The University of California, Berkeley. He is an industry veteran in the realm of robotics. He worked for seven years at Yaskawa in a highly specialized R&D team developing robot control software. He also led the effort to re-architect the Semiconductor tool robot control software at Applied Materials. He also worked as a Senior Software Engineer at Apple developing both frontend and backend software. He has made multiple inventions in the fields of robot motion through singularities and robot path planning, one of which is patented.
